NFL Offseason Catch up: Trades, Free Agent Signings and Tags

Editorial Staff
3yrs ago

The entire league has been somewhat shaken up so far in both trades and free agency. We saw a lot of big names moved around, with veteran quarterbacks Tom Brady and Philip Rivers both landing new homes just to name a few. That has certainly changed the odds in the AFC and NFC as Tampa Bay became tied for the fourth favorite in the NFL to win the Super Bowl at 14-1 (Vegas Insider), while Indy improved to 20-1. On the other hand, New England fell to 30-1 and the Chargers to 40-1. With this article, I will catch you up on the trades and big signings so far during the break. Be sure to come back to us often as I will be keeping you up to date on the latest offseason moves from each NFL team.

Key Trades

  1. Jaguars traded CB A.J. Bouye to the Broncos for a 2020 4th round pick

  1. Jaguars traded DE Calais Campbell to the Ravens for a 2020 5th round pick

  1. Ravens traded TE Hayden Hurst and a 2020 4th round pick to the Falcons for a 2020 2nd round pick and a 2020 5th round pick

  1. 49ers traded DT DeForest Buckner to the Colts for a 2020 1st round pick

  1. Texans traded WR DeAndre Hopkins and a 2020 4th round pick to the Cardinals for RB David Johnson, a 2020 2nd round pick and a 2021 4th round pick

  1. Vikings traded WR Stefon Diggs to the Bills for a 2020 1st round pick, a 2020 5th round pick, a 2020 6th round pick and a 2021 4th round pick

  1. Titans traded DT Jurrell Casey to the Broncos for a 2020 7th round pick

  1. Jaguars traded QB Nick Foles to the Bears for a 2020 4th round pick

  1. Lions traded CB Darius Slay to the Eagles for a 2020 3rd round pick and a 2020 5th round pick
