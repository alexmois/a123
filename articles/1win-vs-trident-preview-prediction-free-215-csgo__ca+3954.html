1WIN vs. Trident, Preview & Prediction - Free 2/15 CS:GO

The Admiral
2yrs ago

1WIN is a slight favorite in this Best of 3 encounter when they take on Trident in the Group A opening match of the Snow Sweet Snow 2 Regionals on Monday. Alongside 1WIN and Trident, EC Kyiv and 100PG are also playing in this group.

1WIN Team Stats and Latest Results

1WIN concluded 2020 with a loss to ex-ETHEREAL at the Dell Gaming League 2020, while they also opened a new year in the same fashion with a defeat to MBAPPEKK at the DreamHack Open January 2021 Europe Open Qualifier. They beat MAESTRO and Unique to win 1st place at the ESEA Winter Cash Cup 3 Europe and $3,000. The Russians defeated Vexed at the ESEA Winter Cash Cup 4 Europe, but lost to OL and had to settle with $1,000. 1WIN won five of the previous nine matches.

In the last three months, 1WIN was invincible on Nuke (played three times) and was excellent on Dust2 (83.3% win rate, while they failed to get a victory on Overpass (played twice) and Mirage (played three times). Since December 2020, 1WIN is playing with the current lineup that appeared on 15 maps with a record of nine wins and six losses. The team members are DavCost (1.02 rating), propleh (1.07), Polt (1.14), TRAVIS (1.09), and deko (1.30).

Trident Team Stats and Latest Results

Trident (ex-Cyber Legacy) is a new team that joined the CS:GO circus in January this year and they are on the tour for less than a month. Their first-ever game was at the Snow Sweet Snow 1 Regionals and resulted in a 2-0 win over HellRaisers, after which they beat Project X, eXploit, and Young Ninjas, but a narrow loss to EC Kyiv prevented them from qualifying for the main event. Trident won four of the last eight games.

During that one month, Trident won both appearances on Nuke and was average on Mirage and Inferno, while they dint taste a victory on Overpass (played once) and were unsuccessful on Train (33.3%). Trident is going to play with a new lineup at this tournament as they parted ways with dukefissura. The lineup currently consists of seized (0.98), clax (1.07), tricky (0.99), and glowing (1.00).

1WIN vs. Trident H2H Record

These rivals have met on two occasions so far, and Trident (Cyber Legacy) won both duels, including the most recent one at the European Development Championship 1 Closed Qualifier in late November 2020.

1WIN vs. Trident Betting Preview

Interestingly, only one of Trident’s eight games produced a maximum of three maps, while that was the case in just two of 1WIN’s last eight Best of 3 matches. Even though I usually bet on Over 2.5 maps when it comes to close encounters such as this one, I am going with Under 2.5 maps this time around because I will follow betting trends and recent results.

The Pick: Under 2.5 Maps (-143 @GG.BET)
