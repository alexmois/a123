B8 is currently unranked in the world ranking. This is based on the fact that the Ukrainian team has been inactive for the better part of the year. The team’s best-played map is Vertigo (55%-win rate). On the other hand, they failed to impress on Overpass (50%), Nuke (47%), Dust2 (25%), Ancient (28%), and Mirage (37%). 0to100 Team Stats and Latest Results

The Macedonian team managed 6 wins from the last 10 games and attained a win rate of 66.7% over the past 3 months. Considering their performance in past events, they finished 3rd at the CEE Champions 2021, 2nd at the Kuvo OneTap Winter 2022, and 3-4th at the ESL Challenger Valencia 2022 Europe Open Qualifier 2. Their all-time win rate stands at 67%.

The team’s best-played map consists of Dust2 (75%-win rate), Inferno (83%), Nuke (66%), Mirage (60%), and Ancient (100%). On the other hand, they were dismal on Vertigo (0%). The team’s line-up has NENO (0.98 player rating), DEPRESHN (1.04), br0 (0.73), HEv1X (0.99), and xicoz (1.06).

B8 vs. 0to100 Betting Preview

We should expect an entertaining matchup when these teams cross paths. At the moment, 0to100 has a better all-time win rate while B8 has a better roster. 0to100 has also shown a better performance on the maps.
