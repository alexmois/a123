Wygers vs. Budapest Five, Preview & Prediction - Free 11/9 CS:GO

The Admiral
2yrs ago

Budapest Five is a favorite in this Best of 3 encounter when they face Wygers in the Lower bracket round 1 of the ESEA Advanced Season 35 Europe on Monday. The winner of this clash will meet the losing side of the duel between Izako Boars and Winstrike.

Wygers Team Stats and Latest Results

Wygers was not playing often in recent months; in fact, they played just six matches in the previous three months, winning only one over ZEROZONE at La Copa 2020 in late September. They are on a three-game losing streak after suffering defeats to sAw, Movistar Riders, and Sangal, winning a couple of maps in the process.

During those three months, Wygers won the only appearance on Dust2 and was decent on Vertigo (60.0% win rate), while they didn’t taste a victory on Overpass (played three times), Train (played once), and Nuke (played twice). Wygers is playing with the current lineup since August this year and appeared on 15 maps with a record of five wins and ten losses. The team members are SOKER (0.97 rating), DeathZz (1.02), Ar4gorN (0.93), OMG (0.94), and SunPayus (1.05).

Budapest Five Team Stats and Latest Results

Budapest Five has played more games in recent weeks and months, but without much success either. They failed to qualify for the Flashpoint 2 after losing to North and AGO, without winning a single map along the way, while they also lost the latest match to Real Betis at the ESEA Advanced Season 35 Europe. Budapest Five lost eight of the last 13 games.

In the previous three months, Budapest Five was victorious in the only appearance on Mirage and was average on Inferno, Nuke, Train, and Vertigo, while they failed to get a win on Dust2 (played three times) and Overpass (played once). Since October 2020, Budapest Five is playing with the current lineup that appeared on 12 maps and recorded five wins and seven losses. The lineup consists of kolor (0.91), fleav (1.11), coolio (0.80), kory (1.00), and torzsi (1.07).

Wygers vs. Budapest Five H2H Record

These rivals have never met before.

Wygers vs. Budapest Five Betting Preview

Although Budapest Five is a better-ranked team and Wygers is not even in the top 100, I am not going to back the favorites here. They are not playing well at the moment, which also can be said for the Spaniards, but the underdogs will definitely have a chance to make an upset. I expect to see a lot of ups and downs from both sides, and that’s why I am going with Over 2.5 maps and higher odds rather than betting on Budapest Five to win.

The Pick: Over 2.5 Maps (+111 @GG.BET)
