SINNERS Esports vs. eSuba Preview & Prediction- Free 3/31 CS: GO

The Admiral
1yr ago

The rivals will play their opening match in Group B this Thursday. The 2022 Tipsport Bratislava Cup is an online CSGO tournament offering a prize pool of $44,585 and features notable teams in Europe. The group stage takes after the GSL format. The opening matches are played in the best-of-one series while the elimination and decider matches are best-of-three.

SINNERS Esports Team Stats and Latest Results

SINNERS Esports is ranked #38 in the world ranking and has been among the top 30 teams for 36 weeks. The Czech team is coming to this tournament on a 4-match losing streak and has won 3 out of their last 10 games. The team reached the quarter-finals of the Thunderpick Bitcoin Series 2022 tournament but was eliminated by Entropiq with a score of 0:2. On the other hand, they lost all their 3 matches at the Pinnacle Cup III and finished 19-20th. Their last achievement was at the Malta Vibes Knockout Series #6 tournament in February where they finished 3rd place and were awarded $3,000. Over the last 3 months, they attained a win rate of 56.0%.

Map win statistics in the last 3 months show a decent win rate on Inferno (80.0%) and Vertigo (57.1%). On the other hand, they failed to impress on Mirage (41.7%), Nuke (41.7%), Ancient (25.0%), and Overpass (0.0%). Their current line-up includes ZEDKO (1.02 player rating), beastik (0.98), SHOCK (1.04), NEOFRAG (1.18), and forsyy (1.13).

eSuba Team Stats and Latest Results

eSuba Team ranks #99 in the world ranking. The team qualified for the ongoing Tipsport Bratislava Cup 2022 based on their performance at the Closed Qualifier. Their last match took place at the Pinnacle Winter Series 3 on March 10. In this match, eSuba lost to the Tricked eSports with a score of 2:0. Considering their performance in past events, they finished 15-16th at the Pinnacle Winter Series 1 Regionals, 15-16th at the Pinnacle Winter Series 2 Regionals, and 6-8th at the Pinnacle Winter Series 3 Regionals. Their win rate over the last 3 months stands at 27.8% and they won 4 out of their last 10 games.

Map win statistics in the last 3 months show that the team failed to impress in any of the cards. They managed 40.0%-win rate on Inferno & Nuke, Ancient (28.6%), Mirage (25.0%), Overpass (25.0%), and Vertigo (20.0%). Their roster consists of Levi (0.98 player rating), Pechyn (0.95), Blogg1s (1.06), sAvana1 (0.99), and desty (0.77).

SINNERS Esports vs. eSuba H2H Record

Head-to-head has 5 meetings and SINNERS won in all their encounters. Their last encounter was at the Sazka eLEAGUE Fall 2021 Finals on October 22, 2021.

SINNERS Esports vs. eSuba Betting Preview

Both teams are currently struggling to win matches because SINNERS lost all their last 4 matches while eSuba has only won 1 out of their last 4 games. But considering their performance on the maps, SINNERS has an advantage on Mirage, Inferno, Nuke, and Vertigo. eSuba has a slight lead on Overpass and Ancient. Therefore, we are predicting a win for SINNERS Esports.

The Pick: SINNERS Esports to win.
