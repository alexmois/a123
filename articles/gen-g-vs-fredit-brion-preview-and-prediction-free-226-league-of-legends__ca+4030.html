Gen. G Stats and Latest Results

Fredit BRION Stats and Latest Results

Gen. G vs. Fredit BRION H2H Record

Their first encounter ever was canceled, as a part of KeSPA Cup last season, so their first encounter was their first of the season 2021. Gen. G was a better team and won easily in approximately 30 mins per match.

Gen. G vs. Fredit BRION Betting Previews

The only chance for Fredit BRION is to survive and wait for the late game because that is the only weakness of Gen. G. Everything points out that Gen. G will put everything in the early game, just to get an advantage in the first 15 mins. They are a big favorite to win this duel but if their early efforts could be stopped, their win could become questionable.
