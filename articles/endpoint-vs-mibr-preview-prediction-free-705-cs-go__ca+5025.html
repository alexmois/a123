Endpoint vs. MIBR, Preview, & Prediction - Free 7/05 CS: GO

The Admiral
2yrs ago

Both sides won their first round of stage 2 and need two extra wins to progress to the playoffs. Each team will play a maximum of 4 matches at this stage.

Endpoint Team Stats and Latest Results

Endpoint is ranked 32nd on the global ranking. They were invited to stage 2 of the Pinnacle Cup II tournament. They played Copenhagen Flames in the first round and showed better execution on Vertigo and Inferno. They displayed an excellent performance at the Elisa Invitational Summer 2021 where they finished 3-4th. At the same time, they won the ESL Premiership Spring 2021 Powered by Intel, and were awarded $8,746. Considering the last 8 matches, the British team has won 4 matches and lost 4. Their win rate in the last 3 months is 60.0%.

Map win rate in the last 3 months shows that the team has been impressive on Overpass (73.9%), Vertigo (64.3%), Train (54.5%), Inferno (53.3%), and Mirage (52.4%). They didn’t display a good performance on Dust2 (42.9%) and Ancient (40.0%). The team lineup consists of CRUC1AL (1.11), MiGHTYMAX (0.95), Surreal (1.04), Thomas (1.07), and mezii (1.23).

MIBR Team Stats and Latest Results

MIBR is ranked 54th on the global ranking. The Brazilian team was also invited to join the Pinnacle Cup II tournament at stage 2. This is the third European tournament MIBR will be playing and based on the previous performances, they are yet to achieve any significant progress in Europe. They failed to win a single match in both Spring Sweet Spring 3 and Gamers Without Borders 2021. Nevertheless, they won their first match at the Pinnacle Cup II which is a step in the right direction. In that match, they played HAVU and won 2-1. In the last 8 matches, MIBR won 3 matches and lost 5. They have a win rate of 50.0%.

Map win rate in the last 3 months shows that they won all their matches on Inferno and Train, achieving a 100% win rate. At the same time, they had a good display on Mirage (62.5%) and average performance on Overpass (50.0%). On the other hand, they failed to register above-average performance on Vertigo (33.3%), Dust2 (25.0%), and Ancient (20.0%). Their lineup consists of boltz (1.06), yel (0.98), chelo (1.13), exit (1.03), and shz (1.02).

Endpoint vs. MIBR H2H Record

There is no head-to-head meeting between the opponents and they will clash for the first time at the Pinnacle Cup II tournament

Endpoint vs. MIBR Betting Preview

MIBR were lucky to get a 2-1 win when they played HAVU in the first round of this tournament. This, however, isn’t a testimony that the Brazilian team is ready to change the poor narrative around their previous European tournaments. They defeated HAVU after 5 consecutive defeats. Endpoint on the other hand seems on point and has a 3 match win streak added with an impressive performance on Overpass, Vertigo, and Inferno. For that reason, we predict that the British will win.

The Pick: Endpoint to win
