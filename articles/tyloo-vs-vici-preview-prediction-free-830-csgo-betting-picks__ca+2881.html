TYLOO vs. ViCi, Preview & Prediction - Free 8/30 CS:GO Betting Picks

The Admiral
3yrs ago

TYLOO and ViCi will meet in the Grand final best of 5 at the ESL One Cologne 2020 Asia (Online) tournament. This will be their eighty-third encounter at the CS:GO maps and TYLOO leads 52-31 (8 overtimes). The upper bracket team will have an automatic 1-0 map advantage in the final, and that is TYLOO. Four teams participated at the ESL One Cologne 2020 Asia (Online) tournament; third and fourth place went to Invictus and Beyond, who won cash prizes of $2,500 each.

TYLOO is unstoppable on Asian soil this summer. They suffered the last defeat on June 25th, 2020, against Invictus. Since then, they have an incredible run of nineteen wins in a row and a win rate of 96.2% in the past three months. During that period, they won the DreamHack Open Summer 2020 Asia, the ProHouse Wuxi Villa Cup, and the Perfect World Asia League Summer 2020 tournaments. From the prize pool, they took home $59,700 in total and 2000 RMR points. TYLOO has never lost 1-2 in Bo3 matches in the past three months.

Their victories were convincing; 2-0 in 73.9% of duels. At this point, they are unbeaten on Nuke and Overpass (100.0%), and it is hard to play against them on Mirage and Vertigo (85.7%). Even on other maps, they have a positive score between 66% and 83%. Each individual in the TYLOO roster has a rating between 1.15 and 1.19. TYLOO’s coach chose these five players for the next match: YuLun “Summer” Cai, YuanZhang “Attacker” Sheng, HaoWen “somebody” Xu, Kelun “SLOVELY” Sun, and Zhenghao “DANK1NG” Lv. Upcoming events and leagues for TYLOO are the Perfect World Asia League Fall 2020, ESL Pro League Season 12 Asia, and the FunSpark ULTI 2020 tournament.

ViCi was pretty solid this summer. They finished second at the DreamHack Open Summer 2020 Asia (Online) tournament, and third at the Perfect World Asia League Summer 2020 tournament. ViCi won five of the last eight matches and has a win rate of 66.7% in the past three months. The most experienced player in the team is a 28-year-old Zhuo “advent” Liang with 0.54 kills per round and a rating of 0.89.

The youngest player Yi “JamYoung” Yang has been with the team for less than a year, but he proved out to be the best one with 0.77 kills per round and a rating of 1.18. The other three who will be the backbone of the team in the finals are Andrew “Kaze” Khong, ZhiHong “aumaN” Liu, and WeiJie “zhokiNg” Zhong. They prefer to play Nuke (87.5%), where they record fourteen wins and two losses. They are most vulnerable to Overpass (20.0%) and Dust2 (0.0%).

Pick: Total maps over 3.5 (-162 @Pinnacle)

TYLOO is a better-ranked team (#42 vs. #68) and the bookmaker’s favorite. They played three matches against ViCi in the past 30 days, they won all three duels but always lost at least one map. I think it will be the same this time, and I bet on total maps over 3.5 at Pinnacle.
