2022-23 College Football Bowl Schedule and Odds

Ben the Pen
9mos ago

The 2021-21 bowl season begins on Dec. 16 with the Bahamas Bowl, featuring Miami-Ohio vs. UAB. The regular part of the bowl season ends with Utah vs. Penn State on January 2, 2022. But all eyes will be on the College Football Playoff Championship Game on January 9 at 7:30pmET from SoFi Stadium in Inglewood, Ca. That game will feature the winner of No. 1 Georgia vs. No.4 Ohio State against the winner of No. 2 Michigan vs. No. 3 TCU.
