MIBR vs. 9z Preview & Prediction- Free 4/11 CS: GO

1yr ago

This Monday, the opponents will play their first match at the PGL Major Antwerp 2022 Americas RMR tournament. There are 16 teams in this tournament battling for a prize pool of $50,000. The best team will be awarded $25,000 plus a spot at the upcoming PGL Major Antwerp 2022. This match will be played in the best-of-one series.

MIBR Team Stats and Latest Results

MIBR ranks #27 in the world ranking and has been among the top 30 teams for 3 weeks. The Brazilian team is coming to this event on an 8-match win streak and has attained a win rate of 73.3% over the last 3 months. The team’s last match took place at the grand final of the ESL NA tournament on April 04 and they defeated Carpe Diem with a score of 3:1. Considering their performance in past events, they finished 2nd at the ESEA Winter 2021 Cash Cup 6 North America and won the ESEA Spring 2022 Cash Cup 1 North America both in March. Since the beginning of the year, the team has played 30 matches and won 9 out of their last 10 games.

Map performance in the last 3 months shows an impressive win rate on Vertigo (100.0%), Nuke (80.0%), Overpass (80.0%), Dust2 (66.7%), and Inferno (60.0%). On the other hand, they failed to impress on Ancient (50.0%). The team’s line-up includes chelo (1.17 player rating), JOTA (1.22), WOOD7 (1.06), exit (1.10), and Tuurtle (1.12).

9z Team Stats and Latest Results

9z Team ranks #32 in the global ranking and has been among the top 30 teams for 2 weeks. Their last match took place at the FL tournament on March 12. In that match, 9z Team lost to Imperial Esports with a score of 0:2. Their last achievement was 3-4th at the ESL Challenger February 2022 and was awarded $10,000. The team also finished 4th at the PGL Major Antwerp 2022 Americas RMR SA Open Qualifier 1 and 3-4th at the FiReLEAGUE Latin Power Spring 2022 - BLAST Premier Qualifier. They won 6 out of their last 10 matches and hold a win rate of 60.0%.

The team’s performance on the maps over the last 3 months shows that their best-played cards are Dust2 (100.0%) and Mirage (66.7%). They managed an average performance on Nuke (50.0%), Inferno (50.0%), and Overpass (50.0%) but failed to impress on Ancient (33.3%). The team has a line-up that includes rox (1.02 player rating), Luken (1.13), dgt (1.15), dav1d (1.07), and max (1.17).

MIBR vs. 9z H2H Record

Head-to-head has 7 meetings between the rivals. By this time, MIBR has won in 3 encounters while 9z Team won on 4 occasions. 9z Team won during their last 3 encounters.

MIBR vs. 9z Betting Preview

Both teams are in excellent form and we should expect an interesting matchup when they meet. MIBR has an 8-match win streak while 9z Team won 6 out of their last 10 games. A comparison of both teams’ performance on the maps shows that MIBR has an advantage on Dust2, Inferno, Nuke, Overpass, Vertigo, and Ancient while 9z leads in Mirage. Therefore, MIBR team is the favorite and will win this matchup.

The Pick: MIBR to win.
