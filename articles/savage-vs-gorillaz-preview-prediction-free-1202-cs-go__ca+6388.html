Savage vs. GORILLAZ Preview & Prediction - Free 12/02 CS: GO

The Admiral
1yr ago

Savage and GORILLAZ will meet at the group stage of the ongoing Elisa Invitational Winter 2021 Regionals this Thursday. So far, both teams have won their first 2 matches. In this tournament, 8 teams will book a spot at the Main Qualifier.

Savage Team Stats and Latest Results

Savage is ranked 48th in the world ranking. At the group stage of the ongoing Elisa Invitational Winter 2021 Regionals, the team has defeated KOVA (2:1) and Lynn Vision (2:1). They won 6 out of their last 8 matches and hold a win rate of 80.0%. Considering their performance in past events, they finished 1-4th at the Funspark ULTI 2021 Europe Regional Series 4 Closed Qualifier, 9-12th at the Funspark ULTI 2021 Europe Regional Series 4, and 1-2nd at the ESL Challenger Anaheim 2022 Europe Open Qualifier 1.

Map win statistics in the last 3 months shows that the team has been impressive on Inferno (100.0%), Mirage (100.0%), and Vertigo (60.0%). At the same time, they managed an average performance on Overpass (50.0%), Ancient (50.0%), and Dust2 (50.0%). The team has a line-up that consists of twist (1.03), PlesseN (1.22), grux (1.03), dezon (1.01), and chawzyyy (1.10).

GORILLAZ Team Stats and Latest Results

GORILLAZ Team is ranked 93rd in the world ranking. The team has defeated AGF (2:1) and Young Ninjas (2:0) at the group stage of the ongoing Elisa Invitational Winter 2021 Regionals. At the same time, they finished 6-8th at the Elisa Invitational Fall 2021 Regionals, 17-24th at the Malta Vibes Knockout Series 2, 13-15th at the Elisa Invitational Fall 2021, 5-8th at the William Hill Cup 2021. They are also part of the Malta Vibes Knockout Series 4 and have defeated D13 (2:0). They won 4 out of their last 8 matches and have a win rate of 56.2%.

Map win statistics in the last 3 months shows that they have attained a decent performance on Nuke (75.0%), Overpass (60.0%), and Dust2 (57.1%). They were weakest on Inferno (40.0%), Mirage (37.5%), and Ancient (33.3%). The team has a line-up that consists of flusha (1.02), JW (1.24), suNny (1.08), sergej (1.10), and supra (0.99).

Savage vs. GORILLAZ H2H Record

The opponents have met once. They clashed at the Spring Sweet Spring 2 Regionals and Savage (formerly Galaxy Racer) won 2:0.

Savage vs. GORILLAZ Betting Preview

This is a definition of over 2.5 maps for me. When both rivals are playing well and are on a positive run, I usually avoid betting on the winner. Both teams won their last 3 matches and have impressive stats on the maps. Since Savage has attained a better win rate over the past 3 months, we believe they will win but GORILLAZ will snatch a map.

The Pick: Savage to win/ Over 2.5 maps.
