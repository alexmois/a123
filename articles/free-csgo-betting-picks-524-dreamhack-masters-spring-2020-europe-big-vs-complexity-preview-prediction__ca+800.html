Free CS:GO Betting Picks 5/24 - DreamHack Masters Spring 2020: Europe - BIG vs. Complexity Preview & Prediction

Editorial Staff
3yrs ago

BIG and Complexity will face each other in Group B of the DreamHack Masters Spring 2020: Europe. Both teams have won one game each and the winner of this game stands a good chance to proceed to the playoffs.

The DreamHack Masters Spring Spring 2020 Europe is a sixteen team online CS:GO tournament. The online tournament has a $160,000 prize pool. There are four groups with four teams each. The group winners proceed to the upper bracket of the playoffs. The 2nd and 3rd place teams will play in the lower bracket playoffs. The tournament’s winner receives $54,000 while the second team will receive $28,000.

Match Preview

BIG won their inaugural match against MAD Lions with a 2-1 score. Despite losing their map pick, Mirage, BIG made a comeback in the latter part of the series. The German players Florian "syrsoN" Rische and Johannes "tabseN" Wodarz led the team’s performance against MAD Lions.

Complexity is not the juggernaut that the organization’s fans would have hoped. When Jason Lake mentioned that they will build a juggernaut, he did not anticipate the problems the organization would encounter in trying to get the best European players to North America. However, despite their relative lack of experience, Complexity’s players have a lot of momentum. They have improved their rankings significantly in the past few months and the team has been steadily working on its map pool.

Both teams have already won a match in the group stage. However, topping the group ensures a direct upper bracket spot, which could mean the difference between a top-tier placement in the tournament or an early elimination. With Mousesports and MAD Lions as the other two teams in the group, this match is an opportunity for either team to take an early lead.

Pick: BIG ML (+130 @ Bovada)
