Quincy Crew vs. 4 Zoomers Preview & Prediction- Free 2/18 DOTA 2

The Admiral
1yr ago

The rivals will cross paths at the Bracket - UB Semifinal of the 2021/22 DPC North America Tour 1: Regional this Friday. This is the continuation of the Dota Pro Circuit organized by ESL. The points acquired from this tournament will determine their qualification to advance to the big event so-called: The International 2022. There are four teams fighting on a double-elimination format for the prize pool of $100,000. Matches are played on best-of-three except the grand finals which are best-of-five.

Quincy Crew Team Stats and Latest Results

Quincy Crew is ranked #11 in the CQ-Esports Dota 2 Ranking and has a CQ.Score of 1743. The United States team is currently in shape because they have won 8 out of their last 10 matches and have an all-time win rate of 80%. The team won the DPC NA 2021/22 Tour 1: Division I tournament which ended in January 2022 and was awarded $30,000. They were also part of the 2021 WePlay AniMajor and lost to VG (2:1) at the Bracket - LB Semifinal. Over the last month, the team has played 1 match, lost, and dropped 4 positions in the ranking.

The team’s best-played map (Default 5v5) has a win rate of 54%. Their current line-up consists of YawaR (78% all-time win rate), MiLAN (61%), KheZu (65%), Quinn (76%), and Ponlo (58%).

4 Zoomers Team Stats and Latest Results

4 Zoomers is ranked #27 in the CQ-Esports Dota 2 Ranking and has a CQ.Score of 1595. The team is coming to this tournament on a 3-match win streak and impressive stats on the maps. They were part of the DPC NA 2021/22 Tour 1: Division I tournament which ended in January 2022, finished 4th and were awarded $26,000. They also came 2nd place at the BTS Pro Series Season 9 Americas tournament and were awarded $9,200. Over the last month, 4 Zoomers Team has been involved in 2 matches, won all of them and climbed 22 places in the ranking. They won 6 out of their last 10 matches and hold an all-time win rate of 61%.

Their best-played map (Default 5v5) has a win rate of 60% and their line-up consists of Zfreek (53% all-time win rate), Moo (50%), Costabile (53%), Gunnar (63%), and Husky (63%).

Quincy Crew vs. 4 Zoomers H2H Record

There have been 20 H2H meetings between the opponents and the advantage of these encounters goes to Quincy Crew who won 13 times. 4 Zoomers have won on 6 occasions and drawn once with the opponent.

Quincy Crew vs. 4 Zoomers Betting Preview

Both teams seem to be on the upswing at the moment. Quincy Crew won 8 out of their last 10 matches while 4 Zoomers won 6 from their last 10 matches. A comparison of both teams' rosters indicates that Quincy Crew is the better side and will win this matchup. Besides, Quincy Crew displayed a better performance at the recently concluded DPC NA 2021/22 Tour 1: Division I tournament than rivals.

The Pick: Quincy Crew to win.
