Sprout vs. Heroic, Preview & Prediction - Free 10/21 CS:GO

The Admiral
2yrs ago

One place in the Lower semi-finals at the DreamHack Open Fall 2020 awaits the better rival of this duel between Sprout and Heroic, but fnatic is also waiting for the winner of this clash. Heroic is a strong favorite in this Best of 3 encounter, as they never lost to Sprout before.

Sprout Team Stats and Latest Results

Sprout is in excellent form at the ESEA MDL Season 35 Europe, where they recorded 11 wins in 12 games and are currently at the top of the league. They haven’t played that well at this tournament as they lost to North and NiP following the opening day win over NiP, so they dropped to the lower bracket. Sprout managed to beat GODSENT 2-1 in the previous round, but it’s going to be hard to make an upset against Heroic on Thursday. Sprout won 16 of the previous 19 matches.

When it comes to the maps in the last three months, Sprout was invincible in 12 appearances on Nuke, while they played very well on Vertigo (73.3% win rate), Dust2 (68.8%), and Train (66.7%). On the other hand, Sprout was below average only on Mirage (47.1%). Since August 2020, Sprout is playing with the current lineup that consists of Spiidi (1.03 rating), denis (1.02), snatchie (1.10), faveN (1.15), and dycha (1.18). They appeared together on 84 maps ad have a record of 57 wins and 27 losses.

Heroic Stats and Latest Results

Heroic was close to qualifying to the Upper bracket at the DreamHack Open Fall 2020 as they had a 1-0 lead over G2 in the deciding clash, but lost the next two maps and dropped to the Lower bracket instead. They had some trouble to beat North in the previous round but secured a 2-0 victory in the end, and now are firm favorites in this matchup with Sprout. Heroic won eight of the last 15 games.

In the past three months, Heroic displayed convincing performances on Nuke (80.0%) and played well on Train (66.7%) and Overpass (62.5%), while they failed to get a victory on Dust2, but played only once on that map. Heroic is playing with the current lineup since August this year and has a record of 44 wins and 29 losses on 73 maps. The team members are cadiaN (1.08), b0RUP (0.96), niko (1.09), stavn (1.11), and TeSeS (1.11).

Sprout vs. Heroic H2H Record

These rivals have met on three occasions before and Heroic won all three matches, including the most recent one at the DreamHack Open Leipzig 2020 in January this year.

Sprout vs. Heroic Betting Preview

Sprout might be in a way better form at the moment, but playing at the tournament of this profile is something else, and even though Heroic didn’t impress lately, they have more experience of playing at this level. After all, they are the No.2 team in the world right now. That being said, I firmly believe in Heroic’s win in this one.

The Pick: (-182 @PINNACLE)
