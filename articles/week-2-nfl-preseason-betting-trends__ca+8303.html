Panthers are: 8-2-1 Over last 11 preseason road games.

Giants are: 9-2 Over last 11 preseason games.

Steelers are: 1)Mike Tomlin is 38-24 SU in preseason. 2)4-1 ATS in Game 2 in preseason. 3)4-1 ATS last 5 on Saturday in preseason.

Chicago at Indianapolis:

Bears are: 1)Matt Eberflus is 4-0 ATS and SU in preseason. 2)8-2-1 ATS last 11 preseason road games.

Colts are: 2-10-2 ATS last 14 preseason home games.

Bucs are: 2-8 ATS last 10 on the road in preseason. Todd Bowles is 7-13 SU, 8-12 ATS and 6-14 Under in preseason.

Jets are: Robert Saleh is 6-1-1 SU and 6-2 ATS in preseason.

New England at Green Bay:

Patriots are: 1)9-3 ATS as a dog in Game 2. 2)16-10-1 ATS in Game 2.

Packers are: 3-6-2 ATS last 11 preseason games.

Tennessee at Minnesota:

Kansas City at Arizona:

Chiefs are: 4-1 ATS in Game 2. Andy Reid is 48-41-2 Over.

Cards are: 2-5 ATS in Game 2.

Denver at San Francisco:

Denver is: 1)11-4 ATS last 15 preseason road games. 2)5-9 ATS in Game 2.

SF is: 0-4 Under last 4 in Game 2.

Las Vegas at LA Rams:

Raiders are: Josh McDaniels is 9-4 Over in preseason.

Rams are: 1)5-1 ATS in Game 2. 2)6-2 ATS as a home dog in preseason.

Dallas at Seattle:

Dallas is 4-9 ATS last 13 as a road dog in preseason.

Seattle is: Pete Carroll is 37-27 SU in preseason., but is 2-5 his last 7.

Sunday, August 20

New Orleans at LA Chargers:

Saints are: 1)0-5 Under last 5 preseason road games.

Chargers are: 1)1-6 Under last 7 on Sunday preseason games. 2)3-7 ATS last 10 preseason home games. 3)Brandon Staley is 2-5 in preseason.

Ravens have won 24 straight preseason games. 2)9-5 ATS last 14 in Game 2. 3)John Harbaugh is 44-12 SU and 39-16-1 ATS.
