DREAM Team Stats and Latest Results

Astralis Talent vs. DREAM H2H Record

Head-to-head stats have 3 meetings. The advantage in this confrontation is on the side of DREAM Gaming, which has 2 wins with 1 loss. Their last encounter was at the POWER Ligaen S18 on November 02, 2021, and Astralis Talent won 1:0.

Astralis Talent vs. DREAM Betting Preview

We are expecting an equal game with at least three maps in this meeting. Both teams have an advantage on 3 cards.
