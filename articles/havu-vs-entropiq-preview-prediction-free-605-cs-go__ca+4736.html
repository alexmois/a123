HAVU Team Stats and Latest Results

HAVU Team is in perfect form this year after moving from position 80 to #21. The team finished 13-15th at Spring Sweet Spring 2 and finished 6th at Flashpoint 3 Closed Qualifier. Over their last 8 matches, they won 5 matches and lost 3 matches. Currently, they have a win rate of 69.4%. That’s not all; they won Funspark ULTI 2021 Europe Regional Series 1 and were awarded $15,000.

Based on the Map win statistics, HAVU has shown a good rating in Nuke (72.7%), Mirage (66.7%), Overpass (65.0%), Train (64.7%), Dust2 (60.9%), Inferno (57.1%), and Vertigo (50.0%).

Entropiq Team Stats and Latest Results

Entropiq Team recorded 3 wins and 1 loss in the qualification stage. They won against Copenhagen Flames, Nemiga, and AVE. They lost to HYENAS. Currently, the team is ranked 15 and expects to improve to the top 10. They finished 3-4th at Spring Sweet Spring 2 and 5-6th at the EPIC League CIS 2021. Over the last 8 matches, they won 6 matches. Entropiq Team also has a win rate of 48.3%.

Entropiq won both appearances on Dust2 (80.0%) and displayed exceptional performance on Nuke (60.0%) and Vertigo (57.1%). They had an average performance on Mirage (50.0%) and Overpass (50.0%). Entropiq has a relatively new squad and consists of NickelBack (1.03), Krad (1.05), Lack1 (1.05), El1an (1.25), and Forester (1.12). The lineup has an average age of 22.3.

HAVU vs. Entropiq H2H Record

Both teams have met on 11 occasions and in all matches, HAVU has won 5 matches while Entropiq has won 6 matches. There has been 1 overtime between the two teams.

HAVU vs. Entropiq Betting Preview

As stated, both teams are in perfect form and we expect a thrilling semi-final. Based on the head-to-head stats, these teams are rivals and anything can happen. First, I expect an Under 2.5 map. On the win, I expect HAVU to carry the day based on their performance in the tournament and their performance at Funspark ULTI 2021 Europe Regional Series 1.
