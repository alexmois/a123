ARCTIC vs. Stars Horizon Preview & Prediction- Free 7/03 CS: GO

The Admiral
1yr ago

This Sunday, the rivals will cross paths at the semi-final of the CBCS Elite League 2022 tournament. The 2022 CBCS Elite League Season 1 is an opening series tournament of Circuito Brasileiro de Counter-Strike (CBCS) this year. This is one of the biggest circuit leagues in Brazil for CSGO community and is organized by Gamers Club and LnK Gaming featuring notable teams in the region with a prize pool of $20,000. Playoff matches are played in a Single-elimination bracket and all matches are best-of-three.

ARCTIC eSports Team Stats and Latest Results

ARCTIC eSports is ranked #69 in the world ranking. The Brazilian team is currently on a 5-match win streak and has managed 8 wins from its last 10 games. The team has won all its 5 matches at the ongoing CBCS Elite League 2022 tournament and requires 1 additional win to make it to the grand final. Considering their performance in past events, the team finished 3rd at the Liga Gamers Club 2022 Serie A May Cup and 3rd at the Aorus League Invitational 2022. Over the past 3 months, the team has attained a win rate of 72.7%.

Map win statistics over the past 3 months show an impressive win rate on Overpass (87.5%), Ancient (78.6%), Dust2 (70.0%), Inferno (60.0%), and Nuke (53.3%). The team managed an average performance on Vertigo (50.0%) but dismal on Mirage (0.0%). The team has a line-up that consists of MaLLby (1.09 player rating), piriaz1n (1.18), ninjaZ (1.02), short (1.08), and history (1.13).

Stars Horizon Team Stats and Latest Results

Stars Horizon is ranked #119 in the world ranking. In the group stage of the ongoing CBCS Elite League 2022 tournament, the team won 3 out of its 5 games to advance to the playoffs. They defeated The Union (2:1) in the quarter-finals and advanced to the semis. The team finished 2nd at the Liga Gamers Club 2022 Serie A May Cup and 4th at the Aorus League Invitational 2022. Meanwhile, they have played 41 matches since the beginning of the year and attained a win rate of 57.7% over the past 3 months.

Map performance over the last 3 months shows an impressive win rate on Vertigo (66.7%) and Nuke (62.5%). The Brazilian team managed an average performance on Ancient (50.0%), Mirage (50.0%), and Overpass (50.0%) but dismal on Dust2 (25.0%). The team has a line-up that consists of GYZER (0.89 player rating), lukz (1.07), UnK (1.05), hoax (0.96), and pigo (1.16).

ARCTIC vs. Stars Horizon H2H Record

Head-to-head has 5 matches. By this time, ARCTIC eSports has won 3 times while Stars Horizon has managed 2 wins. Their last encounter was at the round-robin of the 2022 CBCS Elite League Season 1 on June 24, 2022, and ARCTIC won 1:0.

ARCTIC vs. Stars Horizon Betting Preview

ARCTIC won its last 5 matches while Stars Horizon has managed a 2-match win streak. Considering the team’s performance on the maps, ARCTIC has an advantage on 4 maps (Dust2, Inferno, Overpass, and Ancient) while Stars Horizon leads on Mirage, Nuke, and Vertigo. ARCTIC also has a better position, better win rate, and roster.

The Pick: ARCTIC to win.
