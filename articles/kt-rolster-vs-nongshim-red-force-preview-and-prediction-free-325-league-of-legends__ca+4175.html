KT Rolster vs. Nongshim Red Force Preview and Prediction – Free 3/25 League of Legends

The Admiral
2yrs ago

It will be a direct battle for a playoff spot. If KT Rolster wins, three teams will fight for the sixth spot in the last round of the LCK Spring 2021.

KT Rolster Stats and Latest Results

KT Rolster was formed in 2012, and since then, they are competing in the LCK (one of the oldest teams). They established their current squad last year, but players' contracts will expire at the end of the season. When we compare their solo/duo tier, the player with the lowest rank is Bono, the jungle, who is a Platinum tier player. The others are all in Diamond and Master tier.

KT Rolster is on a negative record this season (5-11), currently eighth in the league. Five victories came against the teams that are on their level. Every time they were underdogs – they lost. If they win tonight, their only hope to enter the playoffs will be a victory in the final round of the regular season. With a loss, KT Rolster will lose all their chances.

Nongshim Red Force Stats and Latest Results

Nongshim Red Force is the team formed just three months ago by the food and beverage company Nongshim. It's their first season in the LCK, and entering the playoff would be a big success for them.

They are on a losing record (6W-10L). They should be the worst team in the league, but somehow, they managed to beat some teams from the top part of the standings. Nongshim Red Force is the only team with a negative K/D ratio in the league (9.8 KPG / 13.5 DPG). Nongshim is the worst team in the league in Gold Differential and Average Tower difference (-1386 per 15 min/-2.1). Even though Nongshim Red Force is in bad form, every time they have managed to get the first blood - they recorded a win.

KT Rolster vs. Nongshim Red Force H2H Record

Before this one, these two teams faced twice, so this will be their third in total and second of the season. Nongshim Red Force won both times, but it was never a comfortable victory.

KT Rolster vs. Nongshim Red Force Betting Preview

Both sides have a great motive to win this game, so the quality of the squad will play a big role in this tilt. I assume that both teams will try to play safe, and it can extend a game duration. All duels they played finished under 32 minutes, but not a single one was this important.

The Pick: Over 34.5 minutes (-115 @GGBet)
