FURIA vs. 1WIN, Preview, & Prediction - Free 6/29 CS: GO

The Admiral
2yrs ago

FURIA and 1WIN play at the quarterfinals of the Spring Sweet Spring 3 tournament. FURIA will debut at the quarters after they got an invite while 1WIN progressed from the group stage.

FURIA Team Stats and Latest Results

FURIA is ranked 13th on the global ranking and has been among the top 30 teams for 127 weeks. They got an invite and will debut the Spring Sweet Spring 3 tournament this Tuesday. They played at the Spring Sweet Spring 1 and finished 3-4th. They are returning from North America after playing cs_summit 8 where they finished 2nd. At the Gamers Without Borders 2021 which was played in May, they finished 2nd and received $300,000. In the last 8 matches, they have won 4 matches and lost 4. They have a win rate of 55.6%.

In the last 3 months, FURIA won all their matches on Ancient and got a 100.0% win rate. At the same time, they displayed an impressive performance on Train (66.7%), Nuke (61.5%), Overpass (60.0%), Vertigo (58.3%), and Inferno (57.1%). They failed to show a decent performance on Mirage (47.1%) and Dust2 (0.0%). They have maintained one lineup since January which consists of arT (1.10), yuurih (1.19), VINI (1.04), KSCERATO (1.19), and honda (0.92).

1WIN Team Stats and Latest Results

1WIN is ranked 44th on the global rankings. They progressed to the playoffs of Spring Sweet Spring 3 after winning 4 matches and losing 1. They won their first match (2-1) at the group stages when they met AGO. Nevertheless, they were unprepared on Mirage and Overpass when they played their second match against Copenhagen Flames. They bounced back to defeat ALTERNATE aTTaX, EXTREMUM, and Anonymo (at the playoffs round 1). In the last 8 matches, the Russian team has won 4 matches and lost 4. They have a win rate of 61.4%.

Their map statistics over the last 3 months show that they have been impressive on Dust2 (80.0%), Vertigo (77.8%), Nuke (75.0%), and Ancient (66.7%). They had an average execution on Overpass (50.0%) but failed to excel on Train (30.0%) and Mirage (25.0%). The team lineup consists of propleh (1.05 rating), Polt (1.06), TRAVIS (1.09), glowiing (1.10), and deko (1.28).

FURIA vs. 1WIN H2H Record

There is no head-to-head meeting between the two sides, and this will be the first encounter.

FURIA vs. 1WIN Betting Preview

FURIA has been predicted to win this matchup by different sportsbooks. Nevertheless, it is unlikely that she will be prepared because they have been invited and also struggle on Dust2, Nuke, Vertigo, and Ancient. 1WIN has a 3 match win streak and has been in this tournament from the group stages. Therefore, we expect 1WIN to progress to the semi-finals of this tournament.

The Pick: 1WIN to win
