AGF vs. Secret, Preview & Prediction - Free 10/7 CS:GO

The Admiral
2yrs ago

Secret is a slight favorite in this Best of 3 encounter against AGF, and they are hoping to start the LOOT.BET Season 8 group stage with a victory. We’re expecting a tight duel, so check out the betting preview for the match between AGF and Secret.

AGF Team Stats and Latest Results

AGF is coming to this tournament after winning the last three games, all of which came at the Dust2.dk League Cup 2020, where they defeated Alpha, MASONIC, and Lyngby Vikings, not losing a single map in the process. They won the first place there and a ticket to the Elisa Invitational 2. AGF won nine of the previous 15 matches.

During the last three months, AGF displayed decent performances on Nuke (66.7% win rate), Inferno (61.5%), and Train (60.0%), while they failed to impress on Overpass (16.7%) and Vertigo (33.3%). AGF’s current lineup has solid experience as they played together on 70 maps since July 2020 with a record of 36 wins and 34 losses. The team members are netrick (0.84 rating), Lukki (1.06), Kristou (1.05), fr0slev (1.12), and TMB (1.11)

Secret Team Stats and Latest Results

Secret was in a fine form of three straight wins over Endpoints, PACT, and Nordavind at the ESEA MDL Season 35 Europe, but then they lost two of the next four matches, including the most recent one to Endpoint at the IEM Beijing-Haidian 2020 Euro Open Qualifier 1. Secret won nine of the last 13 games.

When it comes to the maps in the previous three months, Secret was dominant on Overpass (88.9%) and was fine on Mirage (66.7%) and Dust2 (57.1%), while they were below average on Train (37.5%) and Nuke (40.0%). Secret is playing with this lineup since July 2020 and these five players appeared together on 67 maps with a record of 39 wins and 28 losses. The lineup consists of PERCY (0.97), smF (0.99), anarkez (1.17), Radifaction (1.03), and sinnopsyy (1.13).

AGF vs. Secret H2H Record

These rivals have met on two occasions before, and the series is currently tied at 1-1. AGF won the latest tilt at the Nine to Five 1 in July this year, when they secured a 2-1 victory.

AGF vs. Secret Betting Preview

Not only the most recent H2H duel produced a maximum of three maps, but also the Best of 1 clash before that went overtime and I expect to see another close match with a lot of rounds on each side. AGF is in a better form at the moment, and I am backing them to win at least one map on Wednesday, while Secret has enough quality and experience to take care of the Danes. Still, betting on the winner is not something I’d bet on, and that’s why I am going with Over 2.5 maps.

The Pick: Over 2.5 Maps (-106 @ GG.BET)
