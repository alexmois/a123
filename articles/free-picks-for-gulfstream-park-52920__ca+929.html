Race 7 at Gulfstream Park on Friday is a 5 Furlong claiming for three year olds and up with a purse of $25,000 and field of 11. 1 Good Bye Greg has 13 victories in races of between 5 and 7 furlongs, finished second in his most recent outing last October and will be ready following a long absence. 2 Apache Brave has won four races at five furlongs, finished second in his most recent outing last month at Gulfstream and puts on blinkers for the first time on Friday. 9 Island Tap has won at both five and six furlongs and was victorious here two races ago and returning to the turf could see a change in form. 1 Good Bye Greg to win, Exacta 1-9

Race 8 at Gulfstream Park on Friday is a six-furlong allowance optional claiming for three year olds and up with a purse of $41,000 and field of seven. 1 How Sweep It Is has three victories in races of between five and six furlongs, was fourth in her most recent outing last month at Gulfstream and must not be overlooked. 3 Sayonara Baby has won at five furlongs, finished second in her most recent outing earlier in May at Gulfstream and will be near the front at the wire. 7 Flora Fantasy has nine wins in races of between 5 and 7 furlongs, was victorious in her most recent outing at this venue earlier in May and bids for four straight at distance and track. 7 Flora Fantasy to win, Exacta 7-1

Race 9 on Friday at Gulfstream Park is a 5 furlong starter optional claiming for three year olds with a purse of $27,000 and field of 14. 11 Swirling Candy has seen success at five furlongs, was victorious in her most recent outing last month at Gulfstream and puts on blinkers for the first time for this outing. 6 Tweet Away Robin was victorious in her most recent outing at Gulfstream in January and runs out of a new stable on Friday. 2 Poseidon’s Passion has three victories in races between five and six furlongs, was victorious in her most recent outing in April at Gulfstream and has won three straight races. 2 Poseidon’s Passion to win, Exacta 2-11

The 10th and final race at Gulfstream Park on Friday is a 1-mile maiden claiming for 3 year olds and up with a purse of $25,000 and field of nine. 5 Nimble Beast has placed once over six starts, finished second in his most recent outing earlier in May at Gulfstream and is the horse to beat here on Friday. 6 Wind Ridge has not placed in either of two starts and finished fifth in his most recent outing last month at Gulfstream but has dropped in class here and should show Improvement. 7 Superhighway has not placed in either of two starts, but will be wearing blinkers on Friday for the first time and a drop in class with a return to dirt will help him. 5 Nimble Beast to win, Exacta 5-7
