DRX vs. DWG KIA Preview and Prediction – Free 3/21 League of Legends

The Admiral
2yrs ago

Both teams have secured their playoff spot so this is an important match for both teams. This will be their second tilt of the season.

DRX Stats and Latest Results

DRXis a KoreanLeague of Legendsesports team. They were previously known asKingzone DragonXandDragonX. The only player who is on the team’s roster since the formation is Pyosik. The others came at the end of the last or the beginning of this year.

They are playing moody this season, and they have never managed to win more than two times in a row as their tonight opponents. They have lost their last two duels to Nongshim Red Force and T1. They are in the safe zone of the playoffs but if all other direct rivals win all the games till the season ends it will be tight.

DWG KIA Stats and Latest Results

In sixteen games since the season started, the DAMWON Gaming team has fourteen wins and two losses. If that is not the best proof they are the best team in the league, the roster will clear the sky. Four out of five starting players are Challenger tier players. They are the dominant team in the league and it will stay that way till the season ends for sure.

Win Rate ahead at 15 min is insanely 87%, so it's clear what play style they prefer – aggression from the very beginning. This statline brings two other things. They are taking the first tower in 70% of games, and they got the First Blood in 52.2%. That is the main reason for their current record and standings position. Two teams that have managed to win over them are Ferdit BRION in the third and Gen.G in the last round.

DRX vs. DWG KIA H2H Record

This will be their second encounter ever and the second of the season too. DWG KIA won over in their first tilt pretty comfortably, finishing both maps under 30 minutes, and leaving only five kills to DRX on the first map they played.

DRX vs. DWG KIA Betting Previews

Even though there is no importance of winning this match for any of those two teams, I assume that DWG KIA will win this tilt as easily as the first one. I won’t focus on predicting the winner because it is obvious and won’t pay off that much, so I will put all the bets on DWG KIA’s complete domination because they are superior to a DRX in every game aspect.

The Pick: First Blood 2 (-172 @GGBET)

Alternative Pick: First Tower 2 (-217 @GGBET)
