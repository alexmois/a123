HAVU Stats and Latest Results

HAVU is currently ranked 28 in the global rankings and has been among the top 30 teams for 15 weeks. Even though they have good rankings, their performance at the Elisa Invitational Summer 2021 hasn’t been impressive. They lost their first match to the Copenhagen Flames (2-0) and 777 (2-1). They won the Elisa Nordic Championship 2021 Finland in March where they were awarded $6,250 and a chance at the Elisa Invitational Spring 2021. They lost all their matches at the Elisa Invitational Spring 2021 and failed to progress to the payoffs. This shows that they have a cold and hot streak at the Elisa tourneys. They have also won several tournaments including Snow Sweet Snow 2, Pelaajatcom Series Spring Finals 2021, and Funspark ULTI 2021 Europe Regional Series 1. The current form shows that they have only won 1 match out of their last 8 games and have a win rate of 55.8%.

Over the last 3 months, the Finnish team has displayed a decent show on Mirage (63.6%), Dust2 (60.9%), Nuke (60.0%), Train (58.3%), and Inferno (50.0%). On the other side, there wasn’t a perfect execution on Overpass (44.4%) and Vertigo (20.0%). Since January, they have maintained one lineup which consists of Aerial (1.12), disturbed (0.75), xseveN (1.08), ZOREE (1.07), and sLowi (1.03).

Lyngby Vikings Stats and Latest Results

Lyngby Vikings are currently ranked 49th. The “Vikings” lost their first 2 matches at the Elisa Invitational Summer 2021 against SKADE and Fiend. SKADE won on Ancient and Dust2 and Fiend also won on Ancient and Dust2. Since January, they have been involved in several tournaments including Elgiganten Ligaen Season 16 where they finished 2nd, ESL Benelux Summer Championship 2021 where they finished 3-4th, and the recently concluded where they were runners-up and were awarded $6,500. Considering their performance over the last 8 matches, they have won 5 matches and lost 3. They have a win rate of 52.0%.

Over the last 3 months, we have seen an impressive performance on Overpass (71.4%), Mirage (66.7%), Inferno (63.6%), Vertigo (57.1%), and Nuke (50.0%). On the other side, they tend to lose to teams with better stats on Dust2 (50.0%) and Ancient (25.0%). They have a mixture of old and newly signed players which consists of raalz (1.09), Daffu (1.11), birdfromsky (0.98), maNkz (1.05), and J3nsyy (1.00).
