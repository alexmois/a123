6/12 League of Legends Betting Picks

*All odds courtesy of Bovada*

LPL (China)(Best Of Three)

Royal Never Give Up (-575) vs. LNG Esports (+350)

Spread: Royal Never Give Up -1.5 (-145)/LNG Esports +1.5 (+105)

Total: 2.5 Maps (O+150/U-205)

Royal Never Give Up is the much better team in this contest. Don't let LNG's upset of FPX scare you. They won't be able to repeat that effort here.

Pick: Royal Never Give Up Spread -1.5 (-145)

FunPlus Phoenix (-575) vs. Bilibili Gaming (+355)

Spread: FunPlus Phoenix -1.5 (-145)/Bilibili Gaming +1.5 (+105)

Total: 2.5 Maps (O+150/U-210)

Both team identies marred in fog, I think BLG has some fight under stones of last season unturned yet this summer. A challenge like FPX should be the one to pry it out from them.

Pick: Bilibili Gaming Spread +1.5 (+105)

LEC (Europe)(Best Of One)

G2 Esports (-300) vs. MAD Lions (+210)

Spread: G2 Esports -7.5 kills (-105)/MAD Lions +7.5 kills (-125)

The defending Spring Split champion G2 Esports dominated MAD Lions during the playoffs of the previous split. Look for them to repeat this effort with a big win to open LEC play.

Pick: G2 Spread -7.5 kills (-105)

FC Schalke 04 (-170) vs. Team Vitality (+125)

Spread: FC Schalke 04 -5.5 kills (-105)/Team Vitality +5.5 kills (-125)

Both of these teams were super disapointing during the Spring Split, but FC Schalke 04 had three times as many wins as their opponent. I expect them to make easy work of the horrid squad of Team Vitality.

Pick: FC Schalke 04 Spread -5.5 kills (-105)

Origen (-750) vs. SK Gaming (+425)

Spread: Origen -8.5 kills (-125)/SK Gaming +8.5 kills (-105)

This contest will be super lop-sided. SK Gaming is not a good team by any means. Meanwhile Origen is one of the top teams in the LEC. Expect Origen to take care of business in this battle.

Pick: Origen Spread -8.5 kills (-125)

Rogue (-260) vs. exceL eSports (+185)

Spread: Rogue -6.5 kills (-105)/exceL eSports +6.5 kills (-125)

Rogue beat exceL eSports twice during the Spring Split, but I don't look for them to do that here. These two teams are very evenly matched, and I like exceL eSports's chances in this contest.

Pick: exceL eSports ML (+185)

Fnatic (-280) vs. Misfits Gaming (+195)

Spread: Fnatic -6.5 kills (-105)/Misfits Gaming +6.5 kills (-125)

Misfits Gaming is a super tough team, and they beat Fnatic twice during the Spring Split. I believe that Fnatic will repay the favor in this opening match, however, with a big win over an old foe of theirs.

Pick: Fnatic Spread -6.5 kills (-105)

LCS (North America)(Best Of One)

Evil Geniuses (-175) vs. 100 Thieves (+130)

Spread: Evil Geniuses -5.5 kills (+105)/100 Thieves +5.5 kills (-135)

These are two very familiar opponents in both LoL and CS:GO. They met three times during the Spring Split with EG winning two of those meetings. I believe 100 Thieves will open up the Summer Split with a win here however.

Pick: 100 Thieves ML (+130)

Cloud9 (-500) vs. FlyQuest (+315)

Spread: Cloud9 -7.5 kills (-130)/FlyQuest +7.5 kills (+100)

FlyQuest is a very good team, but Cloud9 is by far the best team in the LCS. I don't expect them to completely dominate FlyQuest though. Look for FlyQuest to keep this contest interesting.

Pick: FlyQuest Spread +7.5 kills (+100)
