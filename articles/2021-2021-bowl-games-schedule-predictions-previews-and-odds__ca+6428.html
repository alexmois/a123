2021-2021 Bowl Games Schedule, Predictions, Previews, and Odds

Ben the Pen
1yr ago

The 2021-21 bowl season begins on Dec. 17 with the Bahamas Bowl, featuring Toledo vs. Middle Tennessee State. The regular part of the bowl season ends with LSU facing Kansas State on January 4, 2022. But all eyes will be on the College Football Playoff Championship Game on January 10 at 8:15pmET from Miami Gardens, Fla. That game will feature the winner of No. 3 Georgia vs. No.2 Michigan against the winner of No. 1 Alabama vs. No. 4 Cincinnati.

Listed below are all 43 bowl games including the date, place, venue, time, teams and current lines from Westgate in rotation order. WinnersandWhiners.com and StatStalt.com will have the previews and predictions available for each game.
