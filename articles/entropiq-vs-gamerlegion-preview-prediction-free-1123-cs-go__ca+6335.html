Considering their performance in past events, they finished 9-11th at the PGL Major Stockholm 2021, won the Pinnacle Fall Series 2, finished 3rd at the REPUBLEAGUE TIPOS Season 2, and 2nd at the V4 Future Sports Festival 2021. GamerLegion Team Stats and Latest Results

Considering their performance in previous events, they finished 1-2nd at the DreamHack Open November 2021 Open Qualifier, 9-16th at the IEM Winter 2021 Europe Open Qualifier 1, 3rd at the DreamHack Open November 2021 Closed Qualifier, and 2nd at the William Hill Cup 2021. In the last 8 matches, they have won 3 games and hold a win rate of 47.6%.

Map win statistics in the last 3 months shows that the team has attained a decent performance on Mirage (57.1%) and Ancient (57.1%) but failed to impress on Vertigo (45.5%), Overpass (44.4%), Inferno (40.0%), and Nuke (33.3%). Entropiq vs. GamerLegion H2H Record

Head-to-head meetings have four matches. The advantage in this confrontation is on the side of Entropiq (formerly Winstrike), which has won in all of them.

Entropiq vs. GamerLegion Betting Preview

We believe Entropiq is the better side to win this matchup based on the fact that they have a better position in the world ranking, have a better win rate, and impressive win rate. At the same time, the Russian team leads on Dust2, Mirage, Nuke, Overpass, Vertigo, and Ancient while GamerLegion only leads on Inferno. Therefore, there is no doubt that the Russian team will come out victorious.
