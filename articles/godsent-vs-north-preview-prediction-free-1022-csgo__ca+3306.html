GODSENT vs. North, Preview & Prediction - Free 10/22 CS:GO

WinnersAndWhiners
2yrs ago

Although they ended the group stage at the DreamHack Open Fall 2020 among the top two teams in their respective groups, GODSENT and North failed to advance to the second round of the Upper semi-finals. Now, they will battle for 9th place and will have to take two steps before winning that position and $3,250 along with 1250 RMR Points. GODSENT is a favorite in this Best of 3 encounter.

GODSENT Team Stats and Latest Results

GODSENT finished the group stage at the DreamHack Open Fall 2020 in 2nd place in Group C after defeating sAw and FaZe. However, they lost to Astralis in the opening round of the playoffs and then lost again to Sprout in the Lower bracket Round 2. If they beat North in this one, they will face FaZe or G2 in the game for 9th place. GODSENT won seven of the previous 14 matches.

During the past three months, GODSENT played well on Overpass (71.4% win rate) and Inferno (63.6%), while they failed to impress on Mirage (20.0%), Vertigo (33.3%), and Dust2 (33.3%). GODSENT is playing with the current lineup since September 2020 and already appeared on 57 maps with a record of 29 wins and 28 losses. The team members are zehN (1.14 rating), STYKO (1.06), kRYSTAL (0.84), Maden (1.06), and Farlig (1.06).

North Stats and Latest Results

North secured a top spot in Group B thanks to the pair of victories over ENCE and Sprout, but just like GODSENT, they were stopped in the first round of the Upper bracket as fnatic proved to be too strong. They also lost the next match to Heroic and now are in a position to suffer elimination out of the tournament with another loss. North won six of the last 11 games.

Over the course of the previous three months, North was decent on Vertigo (66.7%), Overpass (63.6%), and Nuke (60.0%), while they failed to impress on Inferno (20.0%) and Dust2 (33.3%). Since August 2020, North is playing with the current lineup that is made of cajunb (1.04), MSL (1.03), aizy (1.01), gade (1.06), and Lekr0 (1.05). They appeared on 45 maps thus far and recorded 24 wins and 21 losses.

GODSENT vs. North H2H Record

These rivals have met on three occasions before and GODSENT won all three matches, including the most recent one at the ESL Pro League Season 12 Europe Qualifier in June this year.

GODSENT vs. North Betting Preview

I am giving a slight advantage to GODSENT mostly because of the H2H record, but it doesn’t mean North is not ready for the first-ever victory in H2H encounters. Still, I am not going to back the winner here because it’s going to be a tight duel that could go either way. Considering two of three H2H meetings produced a maximum of three maps, I am going with Over 2.5 maps in this one.

The Pick: Over 2.5 Maps (-125)
