Polaris Esports Team Stats and Latest Results

Polaris Esports is ranked #23 in the CQ-Esports Dota 2 Ranking and has a CQ.Score of 1630. The team is currently on a 5-match win streak and has managed 7 wins from its last 10 games. At the round-robin of the ongoing BTS SEA tournament, the team has played 12 matches and won 10. The team was part of the 2022 BTS Pro Series Season 10 Southeast Asia which ended on March 6, 2022, and was eliminated at the Bracket - LB Semifinal. Over the last month, the team has played 8 matches, won 6 of them, and climbed 16 positions in the ranking. Meanwhile, their all-time win rate stands at 68%.

The team’s best-played map (Default 5v5) has a win rate of 61%. The team has a line-up that includes Boomy (62% all-time player win rate), Mac (59%), Nikko (58%), Natsumi- (63%), and Cml (60%).

Execration Team Stats and Latest Results

Execration ranks #50 in the CQ-Esports Dota 2 Ranking and has a CQ.Score of 1510. The team won 6 out of its last 10 matches and has attained an all-time win rate of 51%. At the round-robin of the ongoing BTS SEA tournament, the team has won 8 out of its 12 matches. The team featured at the 2022 Moon Studio Kagura Championship 2 which ended on March 27, 2022, and was eliminated at the Bracket - LB Final by TnC (2:1). Over the last month, Execration has played 10 matches, won 6 of them, and climbed 116 positions in the ranking.

Execration has a win rate of 51% on the maps (Default 5v5) and a line-up that consists of RR (54% all-time player win rate), Tino^ (59%), Palos (53%), Bob (44%), and Shanks (51%).

Polaris Esports vs Execration H2H Record

Head-to-head has 6 meetings between the rivals. By this time, Polaris Esports has won 3 times, lost to Execration twice, and drew in 1 match. Their last encounter was at the robin round of the 2021/22 BTS DPC Southeast Asia Tour 2: Division 1 and Polaris won 2:1.

Polaris Esports vs Execration Betting Preview

Polaris Esports has a better chance in this upcoming matchup. This is based on the fact that they won the last 5 games, has a better all-time win rate, and has performed exceptionally well on the maps. When we consider both teams' rosters, Polaris has a better line-up and has won many matches at the round-robin of the 2022 BTS Pro Series Season 11: SEA tournament.
