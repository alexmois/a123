Both teams will play their first round of the group stage in the ESEA Premier Season 39 Europe. This tournament has 18 teams and only 8 will make it to the playoffs. The top prize for this tournament is $20,000 plus a spot at the ESL Pro League Season 16 Conference.

Movistar Riders Team Stats and Latest Results

Movistar Riders team is ranked 17th in the world ranking and has been among the top 30 teams for 5 weeks. The Spanish team is coming to the Premier Season 39 based on their performance at the Premier S38 Relegation. At the Premier S38 Relegation, they defeated Nemiga (2-0) and Enterprise (2-0) and finished 1-2nd. At the same time, they finished 5th at the IEM Fall 2021 Europe, 9-16th at the BLAST Premier Fall Showdown 2021, and 9-11th at the PGL Major Stockholm 2021 Challengers Stage. They also won 5 out of their last 8 games and hold a win rate of 61.5%.

Map performance in the last 3 months indicates that the team displayed a decent win rate on Nuke (77.8%), Inferno (75.0%), and Vertigo (61.9%). On the other hand, they were weakest on Overpass (50.0%), Ancient (42.9%), Mirage (20.0%), and Dust2 (0.0%). The team has a line-up that consists of alex (1.08), mopoz (1.06), DeathZz (1.01), SunPayus (1.16), dav1g (0.89).

Nemiga Team Stats and Latest Results

Nemiga is ranked 41st in the global ranking and has been among the top 30 teams for 1 week. They qualified for the Premier Season 39 Europe after finishing 3-4th at the Premier S38 Relegation. Meanwhile, they are part of the ongoing Pinnacle Fall Series 2 and have won 4 out of their 5 matches. They finished 2nd at the ESEA Advanced Season 38 Europe and 5-8th at the CEE Champions 2021. At the same time, the team won 6 out of their last 8 games and holds a win rate of 61.4%.

Map win statistics in the last 3 months shows that the team has been impressive on Nuke (71.4%), Mirage (68.8%), Vertigo (63.6%), and Inferno (57.1%). On the other hand, they failed to show a decent win rate on Dust2 (50.0%) and Overpass (33.3%). Their roster consists of Jyo (1.07), boX (1.04), iDISBALANCE (1.12), and 1eeR (0.91).

Movistar Riders vs. Nemiga H2H Record

Head-to-head record has 1 meeting between the opponents. They clashed at the ESEA Premier Season 38 Europe Relegation and Movistar Riders won 2-0.

Movistar Riders vs. Nemiga Betting Preview

Nemiga seems to be on fire currently because they won their last 3 matches. Nevertheless, we believe Movistar Riders will bounce back from their last loss and take this match. Movistar Riders lead on Inferno, Nuke, Overpass, and Ancient while Nemiga only leads on Mirage. At the same time, Movistar Riders has a better position on the global ranking.

The Pick: Movistar Riders to win.
