This Week’s Saratoga Best Bets:

Wednesday Race 2: #1 Lastchanceatglory - Irad Ortiz takes his first mount of the day in a Linda Rice-trained horse. Looking for a play with higher odds? Try the #4 Silk Trade coming from the middle of the track.

Wednesday Race 3: Here’s a large field where you can play a Superfecta Box of 1-2-6-9-10.

Wednesday Race 6: #1 Ichiban - Jose Ortiz returns from injury and picks up a win here coming from the rail.

Wednesday Race 10: A big field for the final race of the day. Let’s try another Superfecta Box: 2-5-10-11-14

Thursday Race 1: #7 Jimmy the Tooth is a strong play in the opening race of the day, ridden by Tyler Gaffalione.

Thursday Race 6: Take the #5 Grab the Glory-ridden Jose Ortiz. Consider an Exacta Box with the #1 and #8.

Thursday Race 10: Last race of the day and its a big field. Take a Superfecta box of 1-2-7-9-10.

Friday Race 1: I like the #7 Mischief Joke to kick off the card with a win.

Friday Race 11: Take a Superfecta Box here of 1-4-7-10-14.
