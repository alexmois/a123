Rivals will play their fourth round of the group stage in the ongoing ESL Pro League Season 15 tournament. This event is one of the most-awaited and features 24 of the best teams battling for the top prize of $823,000. Group Stage is composed of four groups of six teams playing in a round-robin format with the best-of-three series.

Liquid Team Stats and Latest Results

Liquid is ranked #31 in the world ranking and has been among the top 30 teams for 129 weeks. At the group stage of the ESL PRO tournament, they defeated BIG (2:0) and GODSENT (2:0). They have a 2-match win streak and has won 5 out of their last 10 matches. The team’s last achievement was 13th place in the IEM Season XVI - Katowice tournament which ended on February 27, 2022, and was awarded $10,000. At the same time, they finished 4th at the BLAST Premier World Final 2021 in December 2021 and 10-12th at the BLAST Premier Spring Groups 2022. Their win rate over the last 3 months stands at 50.0%.

Map win statistics in the last 3 months show an impressive performance on Dust2 (100.0%) and Mirage (66.7%). On the flip-side, they failed to impress on Vertigo (50.0%), Ancient (33.3%), and Inferno (25.0%). Team Liquid has a line-up that includes nitr0 (0.98 player rating), NAF (1.15), EliGE (1.16), shox (0.97), and oSee (1.13).

Party Astronauts Team Stats and Latest Results

Party Astronauts is ranked #40 in the world ranking and has been among the top 30 teams for 5 weeks. The United States team has been unlucky at the ongoing ESL PRO tournament and lost their first 2 matches. The team is also part of the ongoing ESL Challenger League Season 40 North America and has won 2 out of their 3 games. They won the Mythic Winter Series 2022 Cup 2 which ended on Feb 20th, 2022, and was awarded $4,000. The team also came 2nd place at both the ESEA Winter 2021 Cash Cup 5 North America and PGL Major Antwerp 2022 Americas RMR NA Open Qualifier 2. Meanwhile, they won 6 out of their last 10 encounters and has a win rate of 82.1%.

Map performance in the past 3 months shows an impressive win rate on Inferno (82.4%), Nuke (81.8%), Overpass (75.0%), Dust2 (75.0%), and Mirage (55.6%). On the other hand, they failed to impress on Ancient (0.0%). The team’s line-up includes ben1337 (0.99 player rating), PwnAlone (1.19), djay (1.08), ptr (0.61), and Jonji (1.17).

Liquid vs. Party Astronauts H2H Record

The rivals have never clashed in any tournament and this will be their first H2H encounter.

Liquid vs. Party Astronauts Betting Preview

Party Astronauts started this tournament on a downswing and have so far lost their first 2 matches. They are meeting Team Liquid which is currently the best team in Group C. We believe this will be an easy game for the table leaders (Team Liquid). Liquid has shown a better performance on the maps, has a better roster, and also a better win rate over the last 3 months. Therefore, we are predicting a win for Liquid.

The Pick: Liquid to win.
