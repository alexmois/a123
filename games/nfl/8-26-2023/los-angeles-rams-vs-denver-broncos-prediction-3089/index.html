Los Angeles Rams (0-2) vs. Denver Broncos (0-2) 

Week 3 of the 2023 NFL preseason is upon us, so we are breaking down the cross-conference showdown from Empower Field at Mile High in Denver, Colorado, to get you the best Rams vs. Broncos betting pick and odds.  

The Broncos opened as 5.5-point home favorites with a total of 36.5 points, but that quickly moved to Denver -6.0 with a total of 37.0 points. This is the first preseason encounter between the Rams and Broncos since 2021 when Denver beat LA 17-12 as a 9.5-point home fave. 

The Rams are struggling in the preseason

The Los Angeles Rams opened the 2023 NFL preseason with a 34-17 home loss to the Los Angeles Chargers. The Rams gained just 263 total yards while surrendering 319 to the Chargers, who ran 31 times for a whopping 214 yards. 

Back in Week 2, the Rams’ run defense improved, allowing 98 yards on 25 carries in a 34-17 defeat home defeat against the Las Vegas Raiders. The Rams gave up 342 passing yards to the Raiders, who outgained Los Angeles 440-224 in total yards.  

Stetson Bennett completed 17 of his 29 passing attempts for 191 yards and a touchdown against the Chargers in Week 1. The 25-year-old rookie signal-caller went 15-for-24 in Week 2, throwing for 142 yards and an interception. Ronnie Rivers has led the Rams’ backfield over the last two weeks, notching 15 totes for 73 yards, while wideout Tyler Johnson and tight end Davis Allen have combined for 16 receptions and 158 yards. 

The Broncos have suffered two narrow losses thus far

The Denver Broncos have been on the road over the last couple of weeks. After an 18-17 loss at the Arizona Cardinals in Week 1, the Broncos suffered another tough defeat in Week 2, losing at the San Francisco 49ers 21-20. Denver blew a ten-point halftime lead in Arizona and an eight-point fourth-quarter lead in California. 

Russell Wilson led the way for the Broncos in Week 1, completing seven of his 13 passing attempts for 93 yards and a touchdown. Wilson was 3-for-6 for 24 yards in Week 2, while Jarrett Stidham went 12-for-17 for 130 yards. Stidham was awful against the Cardinals, completing just five of his 15 passing attempts and throwing for 50 yards and an interception. 

Jerry Jeudy had a pair of catches and a TD against the Chargers in Week 1, while Courtland Sutton added three receptions for 35 yards. Sutton didn’t play in Week 2, and Jeudy had just one reception for five yards. The Broncos have amassed 287 rushing yards on 47 carries through the first two weeks of the preseason, and Jaleel McLaughlin has led the charge with 11 totes for 65 yards and a couple of scores.

Full-Game Side Bet

Insiders Status:

Rating:

The Rams lack depth and have shown a lot of flaws in their defense through the previous two weeks of the preseason. I’m expecting the Broncos to continue with a strong performance on the ground, so give me Denver to win and cover a six-point spread in front of the home fans. 

The Broncos’ run D has done a good job thus far, and the Rams will have to lean on Stetson Bennett’s arm. It’s hard to trust the Rams’ receiving corps to carry the team for all 60 minutes, while Bennett is still trying to build some kind of chemistry with his new teammates. The Rams are 0-4 SU and ATS in their previous four preseason contests.
