The New York Yankees and Detroit Tigers will take the field on Tuesday at Comerica Park. First pitch is scheduled for 6:40 p.m. ET.

New York (62-68 SU and 61-69 RL) is starting righty Michael King. The 28-year-old is 3-5 with a 3.13 ERA and a 1.17 WHIP in 42 appearances (two starts) this season (69.0 IP).

Detroit (59-71 SU and 68-62 RL) is countering with lefty Tarik Skubal. The 26-year-old is 3-2 with a 4.06 ERA and a 1.08 WHIP in nine outings (44.1 IP).

The Tigers are -120 moneyline favorites and the game total is 8.5 runs scored.

The Yankees are in a freefall

The Yankees have dropped 12 of their last 15 games, including their last series against Tampa Bay. Can the Bronx Bombers at least salvage a winning season?

New York scores 4.22 runs per game (23rd) and hits .229 (29th) with a .705 OPS (22nd). It's hit 181 dingers (7th) and stolen 79 bases (21st) this season. The Yanks pitching staff sports a 4.05 ERA (13th) and a 1.24 WHIP (9th) with 37 quality starts (25th).

Detroit has had some wild swings recently

The Tigers have lost consecutive series to Chicago (NL) and Houston. There's still optimism in Michigan, though, as the team has made significant strides in 2023.

Detroit averages 3.98 runs (29th) and hits .234 (28th) with a .680 OPS (29th). It's launched 133 homers (24th) and stolen 60 bases (28th) this season. The Tigers' pitching staff has compiled a 4.54 ERA (20th) and a 1.28 WHIP (17th) with 31 quality starts (28th).
