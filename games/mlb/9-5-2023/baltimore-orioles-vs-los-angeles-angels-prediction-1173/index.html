The Orioles are flying high

Baltimore's West Coast road trip continues in Anaheim following a three-game set in Phoenix with the Diamondbacks. It took two of three in that series, holding the Rays at bay in the AL East standings.

The Orioles score 5.00 runs per game (7th) and hit .256 (11th) with a .746 OPS (12th). They've hit 158 home runs (17th) and stolen 96 bases (16th) this season. The B-More pitching staff has compiled a 4.05 ERA (13th) and a 1.26 WHIP (11th) with 58 quality starts (10th).
