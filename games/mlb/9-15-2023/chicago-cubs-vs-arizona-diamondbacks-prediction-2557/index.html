The Arizona Diamondbacks will play host to the Chicago Cubs on Friday at Chase Field. First pitch is at 6:40 p.m. PT.

Chicago (78-70 SU and 75-73 RL) is starting left-hander Justin Steele. The 28-year-old is 16-3 with a 2.49 ERA and a 1.13 WHIP in 27 outings.

Arizona (76-72 SU and 77-71 RL) is countering with right-hander Brandon Pfaadt. The 24-year-old is 1-8 with a 6.25 ERA and a 1.45 WHIP in 16 outings (15 starts).

The Cubs are -135 moneyline favorites and the game total is 8.5 runs.

Key injuries: Cubs third-baseman Jeimer Candelario (.253 BA/.338 OBP/.811 OPS with 21 HR and 69 RBI) will not play in Friday's game (lower back strain).

Should Steele win the NL Cy Young?

Chicago had Thursday off after losing a three-game series in Colorado with the Rockies earlier this week. It's 10-4 straight-up and against the spread with a day off between games this year.

The Cubs average 5.01 runs (7th) and hit .254 (12th) with a .748 OPS (12th). They've hit 175 homers (14th) and stolen 127 bases (7th) in 2023. The Chicago pitching staff has posted a 4.04 ERA (12th) and a 1.27 WHIP (13th) with 67 quality starts (3rd).

Steele will start for the visitors on Friday night. The third-year pro is 4-1 with a 3.00 ERA and a 1.10 WHIP in ten outings this season. In his last start, the southpaw held Arizona to one run on six hits in seven innings. Steele was 4-0 with a 3.41 ERA in six August appearances.

Pfaadt will likely need a lot of run support Friday

Arizona finished its four-game series with the Mets in New York on Thursday. It's going to take a strong finish to stay in the NL Wild Card race with several clubs vying for the final spot.

The D-Backs score 4.61 runs per game (14th) and hit .252 (14th) with a .737 OPS (17th). They've hit 157 long balls (20th) and stolen 149 bases (3rd) in 2023. The AZ pitching staff has compiled a 4.58 ERA (21st) and a 1.34 WHIP (19th) with 53 quality starts (12th).

Pfaadt faced the Cubs in his last start (September 10), surrendering four runs on seven hits, including three long balls. He was 1-3 with a 4.01 ERA in August and sports a 7.30 ERA at home this season (nine starts). The youngster has a 72:25 K:BB ratio in 2023, his rookie season.

Chicago has a serious advantage on the mound with Steele facing the rookie Pfaadt, which leads me to believe the Cubs' offense will provide him enough run support to win straight-up on Friday. At -135 odds, I feel there is enough value on the Chicago ML to support a four-unit wager.

They knocked around Pfaadt a few days ago at Wrigley and will feel confident against him on Friday with their ace Steele toeing the rubber. The Diamondbacks were outscored 18-2 in their last two games in Queens and will continue to struggle tomorrow against the NL Cy Young award contender.

I expect at least one of these teams to break out of their recent offensive struggles tomorrow. While my bet is on the Cubs, it wouldn't surprise me if the Diamondbacks have success against the Chicago bullpen once Steele exits.
