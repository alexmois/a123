Dawn of a New Era

The Cleveland Guardians will likely be playing hard for the last two weeks of the season in honor of their manager, Terry Francona, who is likely stepping down as manager following this season. Francona has all but officially announced his retirement after this season with the possibility of staying in the organization in some capacity. Over the weekend, the Guardians swept the Texas Rangers in three games, damaging Texas' chances of making the playoffs. On Sunday, trailing 1-0, the Guardians exploded for 9 runs in the fourth inning and rolled to a 9-2 win.

The Guardians will turn to veteran righty Quantrill in the opener on Monday afternoon. Quantrill recently returned from the IL and is 3-6 in 16 starts this season including eight quality starts. Quantrill has allowed 93 hits in 85 innings pitched with an ERA of 5.40. Quantrill is 2-2 on the road this season in nine starts with an ERA of 4.93. In his last five starts, he is 1-2 with a 4.91 ERA while allowing 29 hits in 25.2 innings pitched with 14 strikeouts.

Trying to Avoid the Wrong Side of History

The Kansas City Royals own the second-worst record in baseball heading into this series with 102 losses. With just two weeks left in the season, Kansas City hopes to avoid a dubious record and keep from surpassing the team record for losses in a season, 106. This weekend, the Royals did their best to avoid that distinction by winning two of three games from the AL West-leading Astros. On Sunday, the Royals blew a chance at a sweep and a fifth straight win overall with a 7-1 loss to Houston.

The Royals will hand the ball to righty Singer on Monday afternoon. Singer is 8-11 in 28 starts including 9 quality starts. Singer has allowed 174 hits in 153.2 innings pitched with an ERA of 5.51. He has a K/9 of 7.6 and his WHIP is 1.44. At home this season, Singer is 5-4 with an ERA of 4.35 in 16 starts. He has faced the Guardians twice this year and is 0-1 with an ERA of 4.91. Singer allowed 17 hits in 11 innings in the two starts. In his last five starts, Singer is 0-3 with a 7.82 ERA and allowed 38 hits in 25.1 innings pitched.
