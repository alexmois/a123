The Florida State Seminoles are coming off a big win over LSU in their season opener and they hope to make it two in a row when they host Southern Mississippi on Saturday. The fifth-ranked Seminoles of the ACC are 1-0 on the season while the Golden Eagles of the Sun Belt Conference are also 1-0. These two teams last met in 2017 when FSU won 47-13. Kickoff is Saturday at 8:30 p.m. ET at Bobby Bowden Field at Doak Campbell Stadium in Tallahassee, Fla.

Southern Miss wins opener

The Southern Mississippi Golden Eagles got off to a 1-0 start on the season with a 40-14 win over Alcorn State. The Golden Eagles covered as 24-point favorites. Southern Miss rolled up 441 total yards and gave up 226 total yards. Quarterback Billy Wiles was an efficient 21 for 28 for 267 yards with 3 touchdowns and 1 interception. Jakarius Caston led the way with 5 catches for 103 yards and a touchdown. Latreal Jones added 3 grabs for 68 yards.

FSU rolls over LSU

The Florida State Seminoles came into last weekend's opening game against SEC powerhouse LSU as a 1.5-point home underdog. But the Seminoles proved the oddsmakers wrong as they blew out the Tigers 45-24, breaking things open with a huge second half. LSU actually led 17-14 at halftime, but the Seminoles outscored the Tigers 31-7 in the second half to cruise to the easy win. FSU gained 460 total yards, including 359 yards in the air and 135 yards on the ground.

Seminoles quarterback Jordan Travis had himself a day going 23 for 31 for 342 yards and 4 touchdowns with 1 interception. Keon Coleman was his favorite target with 9 catches for 122 yards and 3 touchdown catches. Johnny Wilson added 7 catches for 107 yards. Jaheim Bell added 2 grabs for 49 yards and a touchdown. The running attack was led by Trey Benson with 54 yards while the quarterback Travis rushed for 38 yards and also ran for a score. The defense forced two turnovers, an interception and a fumble.

Full-Game Side Bet

Insiders Status:

Rating:

The 5th-ranked Seminoles were very impressive last weekend in blowing out a good LSU team. They almost look like the Bobby Bowden teams of old with a strong offense and defense.  They wound up winning by 21 points over the perennial SEC powerhouse. They are home once again and taking on an overmatched Golden Eagles squad. Given the fact that FSU ran away from LSU, the Seminoles should be able to score at will against a Golden Eagles team that gave up 14 points to an FCS school in Alcorn State last week. Southern Miss is ranked No. 94 out of 133 teams in FBS Division 1 so they clearly face a difficult challenge. Florida State will roll to an easy win here. Take the Seminoles giving up the points.

Prediction: Florida State -31
