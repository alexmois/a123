What is the Challenge

The Challenge consists in the guess of the exact length of time the National Anthem will be sung before the Super Bowl begins.

How to play

During the Entry Period, any Eligible Entrant may participate in the Challenge. The Eligible Entrant needs the following:

(a) Having a valid account with Twitter;

(b) follows the Winners and Whiners Twitter account (@WinnersWhiners) with their account;

(3) post a tweet during the Entry Period with "@WinnersWhiners", hashtags #SuperBowl and #Contest, and clearly stating the entrant's guess in minutes and/or seconds. Valid examples: "@WinnersWhiners I think the National Anthem sung by #ChrisStapleton before the #SuperBowl will be 1 minute and 59 seconds! #Contest LETS GO!!!" and "@WinnersWhiners 92 seconds #SuperBowl #Contest".

The Eligible Entrant(s) with the correct guess will be entered into a pool from which 1 person will be selected for first prize and 9 others will be selected for a second prize. If no one guesses correctly, the next closest time guessed without going over will count.
